Music

I spend a lot of my time playing trumpet, and have done for several years! In here I'll add details of bands I've played in etc.

In Cambridge I have played with a large variety of music groups. I have been a member of Churchill Jazz Band for the past 3 years, playing lead trumpet during my first and second years. In my second year I also served as Treasurer, and then as Webmaster in my third year while I was at MIT. Next year I'm going to be the band's secretary while carrying on my role as Webmaster at the same time.

Other Cambridge music groups I have played with include

  • CUPO (Cambridge University Pops Orchestra)
  • CUBB (Cambridge University Brass Band)
  • CUJO (Cambridge University Jazz Orchestra)
  • Selwyn Jazz Band
  • Several college orchestras, including Selwyn, St Cats and Queens (Magsoc)

On my year abroad in Boston I played with the MIT sysmphony orchestra, a small jazz band, and on occasion the MIT Marching Band.

Wherever I am I always try to get involved with shows if they have a band. In the past I have played in:
Pippin (CUMTS at Ed. Fringe), Seussical (MIT NextAct), Lucky Stiff (MIT MTG), Legally Blonde (MIT MTG), Into The Woods (CUMTS), CUMTS Gala Night, The Last Post/The Last Hundred (CUMTS), The Gondoliers (CU GandS), Footlights Panto, West Side Story (RGS), Oh! What a Lovely War (Lane End Singers), Oklahoma! (WHS), Bugsy Malone (RGS) + others that I can't remember!
